Join the Drepung Loseling Institute of Texas

DLIT is a higher Buddhist Studies & Practice Center located in Texas historical
site, Dyer House, Houston, Texas. We provide Buddhist Nalanda tradition base
Meditation, Mindfulness and Buddhist philosophical classes as well as Tai Chi,
Yoga Class and others Wellbeing Courses. We have a Vegan, organic friendly
Vegetarian Cafe with a Community Garden. Our Center is beautiful, peaceful
spiritual place on 4 arces with historical.
